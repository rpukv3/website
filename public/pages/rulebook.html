General Rules

Server Guidelines

Stay Updated

Regularly check our Discord for any updates or modifications to the rules. It's your responsibility to stay informed about any changes that may affect your gameplay.

No Erotic Roleplay

Explicit or sexually suggestive content, including nudity or lewd materials, is strictly prohibited. This extends to all forms of erotic roleplay (ERP) to maintain a professional and respectful environment.

Zero Tolerance Policy

Racism, trolling, discrimination, homophobia, and sexism are not tolerated. We maintain a strict policy against any form of hate speech or discriminatory behavior.

Sensitive Topics

Real-life events such as suicide, terrorism, pandemics, and mental illnesses are not permitted in roleplay. We prioritize the well-being and safety of our community members.

Engagement Standards

Player Engagement

Cop Baiting

Intentionally provoking or luring police officers into scenarios without proper roleplay is considered low quality roleplay (LQRP). This includes actions like deliberately colliding with police cars to initiate unnecessary chases or ambushes.

Money Simulator Gameplay

Focus on meaningful interactions rather than just accumulating wealth. We encourage players to build relationships and create dynamic roleplay scenarios that extend beyond financial pursuits.

Cooperation with EMS

Refusing to respond to or cooperate with EMS staff can lead to severe consequences. It's important to engage with EMS staff as part of realistic roleplay scenarios.

OOC Rules

Out of Character Guidelines

  • OOC Discussions: Conduct out-of-character discussions in Discord channels only.

  • Immersion: Avoid using immersion-breaking language.

Communication Rules

Communication Guidelines

Language Requirements

Communication must be conducted in English. While other languages are permitted if all parties are comfortable, English is the primary language for server communication.

Respect for Staff

Maintain respectful interactions with staff members. Combative behavior or creating unnecessary issues may result in removal from the community.

Voice Communication

Excessively loud or offensive language directed at individuals is not tolerated. Staff members have the authority to address misuse of in-character exceptions for offensive language.

Cheating and Exploiting

Anti-Cheat Guidelines

Prohibition of Unfair Modifications

The use of game modifications that provide unfair advantages is strictly prohibited. This includes increased speed, no night vision packs, health regeneration, teleportation, or similar modifications.

Permissible Modifications

Visual enhancements or modifications that don't affect gameplay balance are allowed. Binding keys to server commands for convenience is permitted as long as it doesn't grant unfair advantages.

Bug Exploitation

Exploiting client or server bugs is strictly prohibited. This includes any activities that abuse these exploits. Engaging in such behavior will result in a ban, and any benefits gained will be removed.

Roleplay Rules

Off-Limits Areas

Fort Zancudo and the aircraft carriers are off-limits due to heavy security.

Deathmatch, Revenge, Killing & Kill On Sight

  • Low-Quality Roleplay (LQRP): Intentionally running over individuals with a vehicle, even if justified in-character, can still be deemed LQRP. All actions should focus on high-quality interactions and immersion.
  • Self-Defense and Retaliation: You may attack or defend yourself if another player intentionally tries to harm you, damages your property, ignores your threats, attempts to resist, or takes hostile actions. Responses must align with the context and be roleplayed accordingly.
  • Random Deathmatch (RDM): Strictly prohibited. RDM is intentionally killing/harming another player or damaging property without a valid in-character reason or proper roleplay justification.
  • Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM): Strictly prohibited. VDM is intentionally using a vehicle to kill/harm another player or damage property without valid in-character justification. Even with a valid reason, using vehicles in this way may still be LQRP.
  • Initiating Roleplay: Before any shootout or violent confrontation, you must initiate roleplay with the involved parties, verbally communicate intentions, and allow the roleplay to unfold naturally.

New Life Rule (NLR)

  • Triggering NLR: Activated when a player is incapacitated and respawns or is treated at the hospital.
  • Return to Scene: Wait 30 minutes before returning to the area where you were incapacitated. Do not collect evidence, loot, or engage with the area during this time.
  • Hostile Actions: Cannot take hostile actions against the party responsible for your incapacitation for 60 minutes.
  • Post-Scenario: If downed in a concluded scenario, do not return to the scene for any reason.

Combat Roleplay Logging

  • Disconnecting/Respawning: Prohibited during roleplay or combat situations that could have in-character consequences.
  • Game Crashes: If disconnected, make a genuine effort to reconnect and resume the scenario.
  • Use of Emotes: Using emotes to gain an unfair advantage in combat is strictly prohibited.

Metagaming, Powergaming, Torture RP

  • Metagaming: Using real-life information obtained outside the game for in-character advantage is a serious offense. Accidental metagaming is less severe but discouraged.
  • Streaming: Players in the city may not appear in livestreams of other players who are also in the city.
  • Powergaming: Roleplaying with mechanics or abilities that do not exist, or forcing others into situations with no choices, is prohibited. Examples: forcing outcomes with /me, spam jumping, placing stash/furniture outside visible areas, unfair escorting/cuffing, using vehicles to flip others, spamming attack, dragging to avoid RP.
  • Prohibited Vehicles: Vehicles with unbreakable/extremely small/covered windows or unfair advantages are not allowed for criminal activities.
  • Torture Roleplay: Requires explicit consent from all parties via /me. If discomfort is expressed, scenario must end immediately.

Prison Roleplay

  • Lifers may not break out of prison unless approved by staff. Submit a ticket for approval.
  • Always roleplay as if DOC personnel are present, even if no players are online.
  • Breaking into prison without a valid reason or plan is LQRP. Do not use teleport mechanics to leave unless you are an active prisoner.
  • EMS may transport individuals out of prison for medical procedures not possible inside.

Private Discords

  • All ColMexRP-related Discords with in-character channels must follow ColMexRP Rules and Guidelines.
  • Private Discords must clearly indicate which channels are in-character.
  • Businesses/gangs must post Discord invite links in the main ColMexRP Discord.
  • Staff may require access for compliance; refusal can result in consequences.
  • Provide accurate evidence if requested by staff for reports.
  • Do not remove staff from your Discord unless they are no longer associated with your group.

Crime And Gangs

  • Gang-Specific Gear: Only use gang-specific clothing, flags, or liveries if obtained through roleplay.
  • Gang Roleplay Diversity: Gangs are encouraged to pursue diverse objectives and storylines, not just conflict and violence.
  • Roleplay Before Combat: Always initiate roleplay and use verbal communication before shooting or combat, even if tensions arise from out-of-game platforms.
  • Gang Handbook: All gang info and application process are in the Itrp Gang server.

Criminal Activities

  • 5-Man Rule: Criminal activities are limited to five participants until the scenario is resolved. No outside assistance or intervention.
  • Mechanic Restrictions: Mechanics may not use F1 menu or job abilities during criminal activities.
  • Independent Groups: Separate groups may not collaborate on different objectives during criminal activities.
  • Prison Transports: Attacking a prison transport is a separate scenario; you may assemble a new group of up to five for this.
  • No Fake Hostages: Fake hostages are strictly prohibited in all criminal activities.
  • Robbery Planning: Major robberies require a well-thought-out plan; simply evading police by driving is not acceptable.
  • Robbing Players: Allowed with high-quality roleplay and justified context. Use appropriate commands; no cap on amount, but focus on RP quality.
  • Cooldown Periods: After robberies/car boosts, observe cooldowns: Store Robberies/Car Boosts: 45 min, Vangelico: 90 min, Fleeca/Bank Trucks/Drug Runs: 120 min, G6/Paleto/Pacific: 180 min. Cooldowns apply collectively.
  • Robbing Restricted Areas: Places with 'teleport' options are off-limits for robbing players.
  • Scamming Rules: Must be done through in-game RP with valid reason. No scamming solely for profit/conflict or using unauthorized menus/teleporting (except F2 inventory). No exploiting server functions.
  • Government Employee Robberies: Robbing PD/DOJ/EMS solely for belongings is prohibited.
  • Interfering in Robberies: Only allowed if gangs are on their turf or store owners intervene. No interference for revenge.
  • Pre- and Post-Restart Restrictions: No major crimes 60 min before or 30 min after restart. All crime banned 30 min before/after restart.
  • Shooting Cooldown: 90-minute cooldown for all scenarios.
  • Camping Restrictions: Do not camp/stake out areas to commit crimes. You may check, but must leave for 5 min before returning.
  • Unscheduled Restarts: Wait 15 min after a crash/restart before resuming criminal activities.

Character Guidelines

Character Development

Integrity for Official Roles

Members serving in official positions (Police, DOC, DOJ, EMS) must maintain high standards of integrity and professionalism. This includes avoiding corruption and misuse of equipment.

Character Separation

Each character should operate independently, with no knowledge of other characters' personal information. Avoid creating connections between characters outside the established roleplay context.

Property Management

Properties may be removed after 90 days of inactivity to maintain a dynamic environment. Sporadic logins to bypass inactivity requirements will not prevent removal.
